JESUS CHILDREN MISSION OUTREACH

OUR VISION

our expectation in jesus children mission outreach is to provide foster homes, shelter facilities(Mission House)and schools for over 1000 orphaned and vulnerable children located in the rural background and in the cities arround the world in the next fourteen years.Produce future leaders equiped with innovative sound teaching,wisdom of God,intergrity, sincerity, hardwork,intelligence and Godliness.Train them to live a save and productive live and to play a meaningful role in the Nation by the grace of God.The broad and long term vision is to produce a society that is healthy,safe and less free from all forms of unhealthy vices.

OUR MISSION

Jesus Children Missions Outreach is committed to assisting the targeted children by partnering with with individuals,organisations,and churches to provide for their needs through the shelter,homes and families empowerment project. Activies engaged in,by the mission are important step towards achieving brilliant future for the orphaned and the less priviledge children,in Nigeriaand other countries.

Help the targeted children discover their God given potentials, and give them opportunity to excel in life. The broad mission is that they become shining lights that would positively inflame their communities and the world at large. As they fly high to walk tall,and standout in the crowd.
